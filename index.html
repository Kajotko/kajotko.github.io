About

Hi, my name is Karolina Jankiewicz. I come from Warsaw, Poland and I am currently a final year Design Engineering student at Imperial College London.

I am keen on making things, particularly those that affect our daily lives. Studying in London has helped to expand my skill set which allows me to make my ideas into reality. I am especially interested in improving my use of CAD and Additive manufacturing as well as and coding.

I also love illustration and comics. My work has been published e.g. in I Science magazine.
